About the Artist

Ana Luisa Kaminski Kohn is a visual artist and also graduated in Literature from UFSC. Her artistic production includes painting, drawing, and printmaking, with works exhibited internationally in countries such as France, Germany, and Portugal. She has participated in exhibitions in renowned spaces and received awards for her contribution to art. Her career includes work as a teacher, researcher, and writer, with a strong presence in cultural and academic events.
